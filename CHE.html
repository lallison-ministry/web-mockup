Community Health Evangelism

Our ministry embraces a holistic approach that integrates evangelism, discipleship, and community health education, aiming to meet both the physical and spiritual needs of individuals. This comprehensive model allows us to demonstrate the love of Christ in tangible ways, while also empowering communities to grow in their faith and well-being. By addressing physical health alongside spiritual growth, we can effectively reach those who might not otherwise be open to the gospel, showing the heart of Jesus through action.

One of the key components of this approach is our partnership with medical personnel. We collaborate with doctors, nurses, and other healthcare professionals to provide training to local community workers. These workers, equipped with medical knowledge and skills, are then able to deliver essential healthcare services directly to their communities.

To date, over 2,500 patients have been treated and ministered to through these efforts. Lives have been touched not only by physical healing but by the spiritual care and love they experience through these interactions. This holistic approach enables us to fulfill Christ’s mandate to care for the whole person—body, mind, and spirit—and serves as a powerful tool for transformation in the communities we serve.
