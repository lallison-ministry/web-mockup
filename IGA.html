Income Generating Activity

Income generating activities (IGAs) serve as a powerful tool to transform communities by providing essential training and education that equip individuals to increase their income streams. These initiatives help combat poverty, improve the quality of life, and empower people to support their families more effectively. Beyond personal and familial benefits, these activities also enable individuals to contribute to God’s Kingdom by creating sustainable economic practices within their communities.

Ranging from small-scale farming to livestock rearing, and from savings groups to retail and service-based businesses, IGAs provide participants with a fundamental understanding of finance and entrepreneurship. By learning these essential skills, community members are better equipped to launch and manage their own businesses, thereby creating long-term financial stability.

The impact of these initiatives has been significant. Already, more than 100 community members have benefited from IGA training programs, and the ripple effect of their newfound knowledge continues to spread. As these individuals grow their income streams, they not only improve their own circumstances but also foster a spirit of generosity and stewardship. By giving back to their families, communities, and God’s work, IGAs contribute to holistic development that touches both economic and spiritual aspects of life.
