Emotional Healing Worshops

The Emotional/ Inner Healing Ministry is based on Isaiah 61 and Luke 4:18-19; ‘… to proclaim good news to the poor. He has sent me to proclaim liberty to the captives and recovering of sight to the blind, to set at liberty those who are oppressed to proclaim the year of the Lord’s favor.’

These are interactive sessions addressing past wounds, emotional healing, and spiritual freedom.
